SwapSo

Crypto investment app balancing easy investing with true self-custody.

Timeline 3 Months (2025)
Client SwapSo
Role Product Designer
THE CHALLENGE

Convenience vs. Control

Crypto users face a constant trade-off between easy investing and true self-custody. Existing platforms either prioritize simplicity at the cost of control, or offer full custody features buried under complexity — leaving users unable to confidently manage their digital assets.

THE SOLUTION

One Wallet, Full Control

SwapSo unifies both worlds — providing an intuitive mobile-first investing experience with built-in self-custody. Users can buy, swap, and stake crypto through a clean interface while maintaining full ownership of private keys, backed by a complete brand identity designed from scratch.

Development Process

01

User Research & Competitive Analysis

Conducted 5+ user interviews with crypto investors and analyzed competitors like Phantom, Trust Wallet, and Coinbase to identify gaps in the self-custody experience.

02

Interaction Design

Synthesized research findings into wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, focusing on simplifying swap flows, portfolio views, and staking interfaces.

03

Brand Identity & UI

Delivered a complete brand identity system — logo, color palette, typography, and iconography — alongside the product UI, compressing a 3-month scope into 8 weeks.
